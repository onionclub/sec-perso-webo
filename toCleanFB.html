SECURITY INTRUDER PLAN

CONFIDENTIAL

Date: November 2025

⚠️ WARNING

This process is detailed because the attackers are persistent. Please follow every step in order.

1

Sanitize the Device

Goal: Ensure the iPhone itself isn't spying on her.

FIGURE 1.0: DEVICE SANITIZATION

1. Check for "Spy" Profiles

  • Go to Settings -> General -> VPN & Device Management.
  • Check: You should see NOTHING here.
  • Action: If you see a profile, tap and Delete it.

2. Check for "Spy" Keyboards

  • Go to Settings -> General -> Keyboard -> Keyboards.
  • Action: Remove any third-party keyboards (like Grammarly).
  • Keep only English, Vietnamese, and Emoji.

3. Check for Linked Devices

  • Go to Settings -> Tap [Name/Picture] at the top.
  • Scroll down.
  • Action: Remove any iPad or Mac she doesn't own.
2

Cleaning Facebook

Goal: Kick out the "Night Bot" and remove backdoors.

FIGURE 2.0: FACEBOOK DEEP CLEAN

4. The "Secret Key" Check

Area: Business Integrations

  • Settings -> "Your Activity" -> Apps and Websites.
  • Action: Remove EVERYTHING.
  • Look for Business Integrations and remove everything.

5. The "Rogue Owner" Check

  • Accounts Center -> Personal Details -> Contact Info.
  • Check: Delete any strange Email/Phone.

6. The "Imposter" Check

  • Accounts Center -> Accounts.
  • Action: Remove unknown Instagram/Meta profiles.

7. The Final Eviction

  • Password and Security -> Where you're logged in.
  • Action: Select All -> Log Out.
  • Final Step: Change password.
3

The Telephony Shield

Goal: Stop spoofed calls and lock the number.

8. The "Port Freeze" (Critical)

  • Action: Call carrier (Dial 611).
  • Script: "I need to enable a 'Port Freeze' to prevent SIM swapping."

9. Check Call Forwarding

  • Settings -> Phone -> Call Forwarding.
  • Action: Ensure this is OFF.

10. Silence the Noise

  • Settings -> Phone -> Silence Unknown Callers.
  • Action: Turn it ON.
FIGURE 3.0: TELEPHONY SHIELD
4

The Email Perimeter

FIGURE 4.0: EMAIL PERIMETER

11. The "Hidden Rule" Check

  • Open Email provider (Gmail/Yahoo) on a computer.
  • Go to Settings -> Filters or Rules.
  • Action: Delete any rule mentioning "Facebook," "Delete," or "Archive."
5

Locking the Doors

FIGURE 5.0: FUTURE PROOFING

12. The Admin Lock

  • Settings -> Screen Time -> Lock Screen Time Settings.
  • Action: Set a 4-digit PIN ONLY YOU know.
  • Content & Privacy Restrictions -> ON.
  • Set Account Changes to "Don't Allow".
