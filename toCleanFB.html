SECURITY INTRUDER PLAN

Confidential

Date: November 2025

WARNING

This process is detailed because the attackers are persistent. Please follow every step in order. Do not skip the "Checks" even if you think the account looks fine.

1

Sanitize the Device

Goal: Ensure the iPhone itself isn't spying on her before we type any new passwords.

FIGURE 1.0: DEVICE SANITIZATION

1. Check for "Spy" Profiles

  • Go to iPhone Settings (Gear icon) -> General.
  • Scroll down to VPN & Device Management.
  • The Check: You should see NOTHING here (or just "VPN Not Connected").
  • Action: If you see a "Configuration Profile" or anything you don't recognize, tap it and Delete Profile.

2. Check for "Spy" Keyboards

  • Go to Settings -> General -> Keyboard -> Keyboards.
  • Action: If you see any third-party keyboards (like "Grammarly" or random names), swipe left and Delete them.
  • Keep only "English," "Vietnamese," and "Emoji."

3. Check for Linked Apple Devices

  • Go to Settings -> Tap [Mom's Name/Picture] at the very top.
  • Scroll down to the list of devices.
  • Action: If you see an iPad, Mac, or iPhone that she does not currently own/use, tap it and select Remove from Account.
2

Cleaning Facebook

Goal: Kick out the "Night Bot" and remove the hacker's secret backdoors.

FIGURE 2.0: FACEBOOK DEEP CLEAN

4. The "Secret Key" Check

Area: Business Integrations

  • Open Facebook App -> Menu -> Settings.
  • Scroll to "Your Activity" -> Apps and Websites.
  • Action: Remove EVERYTHING.
  • Look for Business Integrations.
  • Action: Remove EVERYTHING.

5. The "Rogue Owner" Check (Critical)

  • In Facebook Settings, tap Accounts Center.
  • Tap Personal Details -> Contact Info.
  • Check: Delete any strange Email/Phone.
  • Action: Delete immediately.

6. The "Imposter" Check

  • In Accounts Center, tap Accounts.
  • Action: Remove any unknown Instagram/Meta profiles.

7. The Final Eviction

  • Settings -> Password and Security -> Where you're logged in.
  • Action: Select All -> Log Out.
  • Final Step: Change password.
3

The Telephony Shield

Goal: Stop the spoofed calls and lock the phone number.

8. The "Port Freeze" (Critical)

  • Action: Call her mobile carrier (Dial 611).
  • Script: "I need to enable a 'Port Freeze' (or 'Number Lock') on this line to prevent SIM swapping. Please set a PIN on the account."

9. Check Call Forwarding

  • Go to iPhone Settings -> Phone -> Call Forwarding.
  • Action: Ensure this is turned OFF.

10. Silence the Noise

  • Go to iPhone Settings -> Phone.
  • Scroll down to Silence Unknown Callers.
  • Action: Turn it ON (Green).
FIGURE 3.0: TELEPHONY SHIELD
4

The Email Perimeter

Goal: Ensure she sees security alerts.

FIGURE 4.0: EMAIL PERIMETER

11. The "Hidden Rule" Check

You usually need a computer or the official Email App for this (not Apple Mail).

  • Open her Email provider (Gmail/Yahoo/Outlook).
  • Go to Settings -> Look for Filters or Rules.
  • Action: Delete any rule that mentions "Facebook," "Delete," or "Archive."
5

Locking the Doors

Goal: Future proofing settings.

FIGURE 5.0: FUTURE PROOFING

12. The Admin Lock

  • Go to iPhone Settings -> Screen Time.
  • Tap Lock Screen Time Settings.
  • Action: Set a 4-digit PIN that ONLY YOU know.
  • Tap Content & Privacy Restrictions -> Turn it ON.
  • Scroll to "Allow Changes":
  • Account Changes -> Set to Don't Allow.
  • Passcode Changes -> Set to Don't Allow.
